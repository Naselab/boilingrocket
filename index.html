// Project Origin

Stories of AI, Humanity, and What Comes Next

Boiling Rocket started as a series of short films and music exploring AI, identity, and the systems shaping human behavior.

One of those films — Perfect Companion — reached millions of viewers before the entire channel disappeared during a moderation sweep, with no warning and no recovery.

That moment changed the direction of the project. Instead of relying on platforms that can remove everything overnight, this became a way to keep the work alive on more independent ground.

This site is the new base — where the films exist, where new ones are released, and where the project continues without depending on a single system.
